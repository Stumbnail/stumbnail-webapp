Stumbnail Placeholder Image Generator

This tool generates placeholder images for the login page. Right-click on any image and "Save As" to download.

Instructions:

  1. Click "Generate Thumbnails" and "Generate Avatars"
  2. Right-click each image and select "Save Image As..."
  3. Save thumbnails as: thumb1.jpg through thumb15.jpg in public/assets/thumbnails/
  4. Save avatars as: user1.jpg through user4.jpg in public/assets/avatars/
